Description

The Student Activity Management System (SAMS) is a comprehensive web-based platform designed to manage and streamline student curricular activities. The system enables students to access their ERP-ID, view their marks, achievements, and other academic records. SAMS aims to enhance the overall academic experience by providing a centralized, user-friendly interface for both students and administrators.
